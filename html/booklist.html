Epic Fantasy Fiction

The Wandering Inn (webserial) by Pirataba

The Wandering Inn is an epic fantasy isekai (other world) with a twist. A young adult from Wisconsin walks through her bathroom doorway only to find herself in a world that is not her own, face-to-face with a "D-D-DRAGON!?!". After running for her life, and finding refuge in an abandon inn, there isn't much for her to do but clean the place up and take stock of her supplies. That done, its finally time to sleep, until a loud dispassionate voice sounds in her head saying

"Class obtained [Innkeeper]!"
"[Innkeeper: level 1]!"
"[Skill Obtained: Lesser Cleaning]!"

What kind of world did she find herself in, and can she survive this crazy world of [Classes], [Levels], and magic, and a mere [Innkeeper]?

Cosmere (series) by Brandon Sanderson

The Cosmere is a fictional universe invented by author, Brandon Sanderson. Boasting more than 23 books or 3.7 million words, spanning multiple series' and stand alone books, Sanderson has just scratched the surface of what he intends to write.

Immerse yourself in a universe with a myriad of unique worlds, cultures, characters, and magic systems, as the main characters in each story learn to overcome their weaknesses to stand against darkness, evil, and calamity - where sometimes the fate of their entire world hangs in the balance.

He Who Fights With Monsters (webserial) by Shirtaloon

In this "light hearted" isekai series, a young Japanese-Australian wakes up in a cursed magic hedge-maze, with no idea how he got there, or how he is going to survive the evil giant hamsters that are chasing him.

Follow the story of Jason Asano as he uses every bit or wit, bravery, hard work, and popculture references at his disposal to try and find his way in this strange and glorious new world.

Teen Fiction

Cradle (series) by Will Wight

This 12 book series follows the young protagonist, We Shi Lindon, as he journeys to change fate after a being came down from the heavens and showed him his future. He has only 30 years to become powerful enough to stop a titan of rock and stone from stepping over the ring of mountains surrounding the valley he calls home, and wiping it off the map. Unfortunately, even though everyone is this world has Madra, a magic forged from their very soul, he doesn't have any. How is this poor 16 year old boy supposed to stop the end of the world if he can't even win a fight against the magic of his village's 8 year old warriors?

Heroes of Olympus (series) by Rick Riordan

Heroes of Olympus is the sequel series to Rick Riordan's hit series, Percy Jackson. This series offers a strange new twist, where instead of following the greek gods and their demigod offspring, in "Camp Halfblood", it follows the gods as they are torn between their greek and roman identities. Olympus is under lockdown and the gods minds are fracturing. But to make matters worse, Percy is missing, and there's this new kid with amnesia and the power of lightning!

Can Jason Grace, and friends, figure out who he is, find the missing Percy Jackson, and figure out what is wrong with Olympus before the world itself comes to an end?

Dragonwatch (series) by Brandon Mull

In this sequel series to the hit-series Fablehaven, follow the story of Seth and Kendra Sorensen as they become the new keepers of Wyrmroost, in attempts to strengthen the magic containing the dragons and keeping them from destroying the world. They are the only option, but can two teenagers really run a dragon sanctuary? Do they have what it takes to even hold a conversation with Celebrant, the King of Dragons?
